Privacy & Security

MULTI-FACTOR AUTHENTICATION

Security Keys

RECOMMENDED

Security Keys are the most secure form of authentication, we also recommend adding a backup security key.

Authenticator App

Authenticator apps are the second best thing for authentication, it is a lot better than email authentication.

OneTap Auth

OneTap sends a login request to your phone and you just confirm it's you trying to log in and that's all. It's the most
easiest form of authenticaton.

Email Authentication

NOT RECOMMENDED

We do not recommend using Email Authentication as that would mean that if someone hacks into your email, they will get access
to your VikkiVuk ID.
HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

Share my data with apps using VikkiVuk Storage

This setting shares connections, 2fa methods etc... rather than just sharing the basic information (email, profile, dob). For example
Wolfie uses those to customize user experience, if you were to disable this setting you would have to manually enter data.

Use data to improve our services

This setting allows us to use and process the information about how you navigate and use our services for analytical purposes
For example, it allows us to include you in the new features we test.

Use data to make our services work

We need to store and process some data in order to provide you the basics of our services, such as your email, connections, etc...
By using VikkiVuk IDs and our services you allow us to provide this basic service. You can stop this by deleting your ID.
MY DATA Here you can delete or request a copy of your data. In your data package you will not receive data from VikkiVuk ID Storage, but by deleting
your data you will delete the data from VikkiVuk ID Storage FOREVER.
